Ecstasy

1. an overwhelming feeling of great happiness or joyful excitement.
"there was a look of ecstasy on his face"

2. a state of extreme happiness
"My father was in ecstasy when I won my scholarship."

Synonyms
rapture, bliss, euphoria, joy, delight, exhilaration.
Antonyms
despair, misery, sadness, torment, distress, agony.
